Spaghetti Popular dish

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic slices and saut√© until golden brown, being careful not to burn them.
  3. Add the red pepper flakes and stir for 30 seconds to release their flavor.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked and drained, add it to the skillet with the garlic and oil mixture. Toss to coat the pasta well.
  5. If the pasta seems dry, add a little reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  6. Season with salt to taste, then sprinkle with fresh parsley and Parmesan (if using).
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Notes:

This dish is simple yet flavorful. The garlic and olive oil make for a rich base, while the red pepper flakes add a hint of spice. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!
